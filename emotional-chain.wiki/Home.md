## Core Concepts

### Proof of Emotion
Validators prove legitimacy through real-time biometric data (heart rate, stress levels, emotional state).

### EMO Token
- Maximum supply: 100M
- Block reward: 50 EMO (halving every 4 years)
- Staking APY: 12%

### Validator Model
- 21 global validators
- Permanent + Tiered device status (online/offline)
- Dynamic reward adjustments
